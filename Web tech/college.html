About Our Institution

A Century of Heritage & Excellence

Vaish College of Engineering (VCE), Rohtak, was established in the year 1995 under the aegis of the Vaish Education Society. It proudly stands as one of the very first self-financing engineering colleges in the state of Haryana. The institute is fully approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak.

The parent organization, Vaish Education Society, has an incredibly rich history, coming into existence in the year 1919. The foundation stone of its first institution was laid by the Father of the Nation, Mahatma Gandhi, on February 16, 1921. Today, VCE carries this profound legacy forward, operating on a delightfully landscaped 16.77-acre campus, which is part of the massive 86-acre Vaish Educational Complex located near the Rohtak Railway Station.
